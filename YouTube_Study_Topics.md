## Suggested study order on YouTube

If you are short on time, prioritize in this order:

1. **Python basics** (Chapter 1) — lists, functions, loops

4. **Confusion matrix, ROC-AUC** (Chapter 3) — evaluate classifiers  
5. **Train/test split & cross-validation** (Chapters 2–4) — honest evaluation  
6. **Decision trees & Random Forest** (Chapters 3–4) — strong tabular models  
7. **Imbalanced data & SMOTE** (Chapter 4) — real-world classification  
8. **Hyperparameter tuning (GridSearch)** (Chapter 4) — improve model performance
